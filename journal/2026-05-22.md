The first week of commuting with a bike has been both challenging and rewarding.
From 5am starts in the rain to vibrant afternoons the spectrum has been wide,
perhaps cycling to work at the beginning of a Melbourne winter isn't the warmest
idea.

The being said I have already felt my fitness improve over the last week, this
hopefully will translate into an improved pace in the next few weeks.
